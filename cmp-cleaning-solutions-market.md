<p><p>CMP Cleaning Solutions encompass two main market types: acidic and basic. Acidic cleaners utilize low pH formulations to effectively remove metal oxides, organic residues, and other contaminants from semiconductor surfaces, crucial for maintaining device performance. In contrast, basic cleaners, with higher pH levels, are designed to dissolve organic materials and assist in the removal of particles from wafers. Both market types play essential roles in the chemical mechanical polishing process, ensuring optimal cleanliness and functionality in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
