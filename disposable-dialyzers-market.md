<p><strong>The Disposable Dialyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable dialyzers market is segmented into low flux and high flux membranes. Low flux dialyzers are designed for patients with stable kidney function and typically remove smaller molecules and toxins, making them suitable for those requiring basic treatment. High flux dialyzers allow for greater solute clearance by facilitating the removal of larger molecules, thus accommodating patients with more advanced kidney disease. The choice between the two types depends on patient needs, clinical settings, and treatment objectives.</p></p>
