<p><p>The industrial frying system market is primarily categorized into batch and continuous types. Batch frying systems process predetermined quantities of food at once, allowing greater flexibility for varying recipes and ingredients. This method is often used for smaller production runs. In contrast, continuous frying systems operate non-stop, efficiently handling large volumes of food. They offer consistent heating and uniform results, making them ideal for high-demand production environments. Both systems cater to different operational needs in the food industry.</p></p>
