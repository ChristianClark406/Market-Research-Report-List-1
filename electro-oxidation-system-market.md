<p><strong>The Electro-oxidation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Electrolytic Oxidation</li><li>Indirect Electrolytic Oxidation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-oxidation system market can be segmented into two main types: direct electrolytic oxidation and indirect electrolytic oxidation. Direct electrolytic oxidation involves applying electrical current directly to the waste stream, facilitating the breakdown of organic contaminants through electrode reactions. In contrast, indirect electrolytic oxidation utilizes an intermediate oxidant generated through electrolysis, which subsequently reacts with pollutants. These methods offer efficient treatment of wastewater and industrial effluents, each tailored to specific applications and pollutant characteristics.</p></p>
