<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes various flaw detection methods to ensure safety and integrity in aircraft components. Ultrasonic testing employs high-frequency sound waves to identify internal defects. Eddy current testing uses electromagnetic induction to detect surface and near-surface flaws in conductive materials. Magnetic testing identifies surface defects in ferromagnetic materials through magnetic fields. Laser testing provides precise measurements and fault detection with non-contact methods. Other methods may include visual inspection and advanced imaging techniques, enhancing overall safety in aerospace applications.</p></p>
