<p><strong>The Audio Transducers and Buzzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio transducers convert electrical energy into sound and are categorized mainly into electromagnetic and piezoelectric types. Electromagnetic transducers use an electric current to create a magnetic field, driving a diaphragm to produce sound, commonly found in speakers and traditional buzzers. Piezoelectric transducers utilize materials that change shape when an electric field is applied, generating sound vibrations; these are compact, efficient, and often used in applications like alarms and electronic devices, offering distinct benefits in size and power consumption.</p></p>
