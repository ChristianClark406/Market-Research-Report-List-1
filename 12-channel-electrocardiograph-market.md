<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter ECG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 12 Channel Electrocardiograph market encompasses various types of ECG devices designed for different diagnostic purposes. Resting ECGs capture the heart's electrical activity while at rest, providing immediate insights into heart health. Stress ECGs assess heart function under physical exertion, helping identify exercise-induced cardiac issues. Holter ECGs continuously monitor heart activity over 24 to 48 hours, allowing for the detection of irregularities not visible during brief tests. Together, these types enhance comprehensive cardiac diagnostics and patient care.</p></p>
