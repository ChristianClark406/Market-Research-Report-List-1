<p><p>The automotive leaf spring suspension market is primarily categorized into multi-leaf and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of several layers of steel that provide enhanced load-bearing capacity, stability, and durability, commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and buses. In contrast, mono-leaf springs comprise a single, thick layer, offering weight savings and a simplified design, typically found in lighter vehicles. Each type serves distinct requirements in terms of performance, load management, and vehicle dynamics.</p></p>
