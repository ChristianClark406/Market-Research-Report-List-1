Rotary pneumatic cylinders convert air pressure into rotational motion, making them ideal for applications requiring precise angular movement. In contrast, semi-rotary pneumatic cylinders allow limited rotation, often used for applications needing controlled rotation without full circular motion. Both types serve distinct purposes across industries, enhancing automation and efficiency in processes such as material handling, assembly, and packaging operations.</p></p>
