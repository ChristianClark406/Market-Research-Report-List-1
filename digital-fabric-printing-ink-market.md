<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Digital Fabric Printing Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Dye Inks</li><li>Acidic Ink</li><li>Paint Ink</li><li>Dispersion & Sublimation Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital fabric printing ink market encompasses several types: </p><p>1. **Reactive Dye Inks** are suitable for printing on cellulose fibers, creating vibrant colors that bond chemically with the fabric. </p><p>2. **Acidic Inks** are ideal for protein-based fibers like silk and wool, offering bright hues and excellent wash fastness. </p><p>3. **Paint Inks** consist of pigments suspended in a binder, suitable for various textiles with a matte finish. </p><p>4. **Disperse and Sublimation Inks** work best on polyester fabrics, where heat transfer converts the ink into gas, embedding colors deeply into the fibers.</p></p>
