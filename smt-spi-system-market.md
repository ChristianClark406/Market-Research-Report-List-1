<p><p>The SMT-SPI system market is divided into two main types: in-line SPI systems and off-line SPI systems. In-line SPI systems are integrated within the production line, allowing for real-time monitoring and feedback during the solder paste printing process, enhancing efficiency and reducing defects. In contrast, off-line SPI systems operate independently of the production line, allowing for batch testing and detailed analysis of solder paste applications, typically used for quality control and process improvement purposes in manufacturing workflows.</p></p>
