<p><ul><li>Analog Type Airflow Meters</li><li>Digital Type Airflow Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airflow meters market encompasses two primary types: analog and digital. Analog type airflow meters use mechanical systems, such as rotating elements or manometers, to measure airflow through visual indicators. They offer simplicity and cost-effectiveness but may lack precision. Conversely, digital type airflow meters utilize electronic sensors and digital displays to provide accurate, real-time measurements. They often feature advanced data logging and connectivity options, making them suitable for modern applications in industries requiring precise airflow monitoring and analysis.</p></p>
