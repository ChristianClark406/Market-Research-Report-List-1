<p><p>The laryngeal stents market primarily features two types: hood laryngeal stents and tubular laryngeal stents. Hood laryngeal stents are designed to provide support to the laryngeal structure while maintaining the airway, often used after surgery or injury. Tubular laryngeal stents, on the other hand, facilitate airflow through a more elongated design, suitable for patients with chronic airway obstruction. Both types are crucial in various medical applications, enhancing patient outcomes by ensuring proper airway management and preventing complications.</p></p>
