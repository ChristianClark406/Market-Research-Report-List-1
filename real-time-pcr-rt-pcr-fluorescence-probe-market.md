<p><ul><li>Taqman</li><li>Molecular Beacons</li><li>Minorgroove Binder Oli-godeoxynucleotide Conjugate (MGB-ODN)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Real-time PCR (RT-PCR) fluorescence probe market encompasses various types such as TaqMan probes, Molecular Beacons, and Minor Groove Binder Oligodeoxynucleotide Conjugates (MGB-ODN). TaqMan probes utilize a fluorescence resonance energy transfer mechanism for quantification. Molecular Beacons are designed to bind target sequences with a unique stem-loop structure that emits fluorescence upon hybridization. MGB-ODNs enhance specificity and sensitivity by targeting specific DNA sequences closely and are effective in detecting low-abundance targets, making them integral for diagnostics and research applications.</p></p>
