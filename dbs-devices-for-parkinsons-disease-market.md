<p><p>DBS devices for Parkinson's Disease are classified into single-channel and dual-channel systems. Single-channel DBS targets one area of the brain, providing more focused stimulation, which can be effective for certain patients. In contrast, dual-channel DBS allows stimulation of two distinct brain regions simultaneously, offering greater flexibility and potentially improved symptom management for complex cases. The choice between these systems depends on individual patient needs and the specific symptoms being treated, influencing therapeutic outcomes and device adoption in the market.</p></p>
