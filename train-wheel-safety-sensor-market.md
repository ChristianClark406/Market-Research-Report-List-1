<p><p>The Train Wheel Safety Sensor market includes two primary types: Single Wheel Sensors and Double Wheel Sensors. Single Wheel Sensors are designed to monitor individual wheels, providing precise data on wheel performance and detecting issues early. In contrast, Double Wheel Sensors assess the condition of connected wheelsets, offering broader insights into wheel pair dynamics and overall safety. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing train safety by ensuring timely maintenance and reducing the risk of wheel-related failures during operations.</p></p>
