<p><strong>The Balloon Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Balloon Catheter</li><li>Cutting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Scoring Balloon Catheter</li><li>Drug Eluting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Stent Graft Balloon Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The balloon catheter market includes various types designed for specific medical applications. Normal balloon catheters are used for dilation of narrowed vessels. Cutting balloon catheters have blades that aid in plaque removal during angioplasty. Scoring balloon catheters feature scored surfaces to help in vessel dilation. Drug-eluting balloon catheters deliver medication to prevent restenosis. Stent graft balloon catheters are used for deploying stent grafts in vascular repairs. These varying types cater to diverse therapeutic needs in cardiovascular interventions.</p></p>
