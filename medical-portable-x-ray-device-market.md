<p><p>The medical portable X-ray device market is primarily divided into two types: analog X-ray and digital X-ray systems. Analog X-ray devices capture images on traditional film, offering straightforward functionality but requiring more time for image processing and often have limitations in image quality. In contrast, digital X-ray devices utilize advanced sensors to produce high-quality images quickly, allowing for immediate viewing and analysis. Digital systems enhance workflow efficiency and improve diagnostic capabilities, driving their growing adoption in healthcare settings.</p></p>
