<p><p>The Endometrial Ablation Equipment Market comprises various types of technologies used to treat abnormal uterine bleeding. Radiofrequency ablation utilizes heat generated by radiowaves to destroy endometrial tissue. Cryoablation employs freezing temperatures for tissue destruction, while hydrothermal ablation uses heated fluid. Thermal balloon ablation involves inserting a balloon filled with heated fluid into the uterus. Hysteroscopic ablation utilizes a hysteroscope for direct visualization and treatment. Other methods may include laser or microwave approaches. Each technique offers different benefits and suitability for patients.</p></p>
