<p><p>The Whole Transcriptomics Sequencing market consists of three main types: PCR, Microarray, and Sequencing technologies. PCR amplifies specific RNA sequences, enabling detailed analysis of targeted genes. Microarrays allow simultaneous profiling of thousands of genes by hybridizing RNA samples to complementary probes on a chip, providing a broad overview of expression levels. Sequencing, particularly RNA-seq, offers comprehensive insights by determining the exact nucleotide sequences of transcripts, revealing not only expression levels but also splice variants and novel transcripts.</p></p>
