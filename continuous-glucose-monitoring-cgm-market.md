The market encompasses several key components: transmitters, which send glucose data from sensors to devices; sensors that measure blood glucose levels continuously; and insulin pumps that deliver precise insulin dosages based on glucose readings. These integrated technologies provide patients with actionable insights, improving glycemic control and reducing the risk of complications. The synergy of transmitters, sensors, and pumps enhances diabetes management and patient quality of life.</p></p>
