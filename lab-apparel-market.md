<p><ul><li>Sterile Type</li><li>Non-sterile Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lab apparel market consists of two primary types: sterile and non-sterile. Sterile lab apparel is designed for environments requiring aseptic conditions, such as hospitals and cleanrooms, ensuring that contamination is minimized. This includes gowns, gloves, and masks that undergo sterilization processes. Non-sterile lab apparel, on the other hand, is used in less stringent environments, such as research labs and universities, where the risk of contamination is lower. It encompasses lab coats, scrubs, and protective footwear. Both types cater to specific safety and operational needs in laboratory settings.</p></p>
