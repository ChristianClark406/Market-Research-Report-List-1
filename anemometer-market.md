<p><strong>The Anemometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Velocity Anemometer</li><li>Pressure Anemometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anemometer market is primarily divided into two main types: velocity anemometers and pressure anemometers. Velocity anemometers measure the speed of air flow, typically using methods like thermal or mechanical sensing, making them ideal for applications in meteorology and HVAC systems. Pressure anemometers, on the other hand, utilize differential pressure measurement across an obstruction to determine air velocity. Both types are critical in various industries, ensuring efficient ventilation, climate control, and environmental monitoring to maintain air quality and system performance.</p></p>
