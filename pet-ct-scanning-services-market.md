<p><p>The PET-CT scanning services market encompasses two key technologies: Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and X-Ray Computed Tomography (CT). PET scanners detect metabolic processes through radiotracers, offering insights into cellular activity, while CT scanners provide detailed anatomical images using X-ray technology. Combining these modalities enhances diagnostic accuracy, enabling clinicians to evaluate both metabolic and structural information in a single scan. This synergy improves patient management in oncology, cardiology, and neurology, driving growth in the overall diagnostic imaging market.</p></p>
