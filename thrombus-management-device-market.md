<p><p>The thrombus management device market includes several specialized device types aimed at addressing thrombosis in various vascular systems. Neurovascular embolectomy devices are designed for removing clots in the brain's blood vessels. Embolectomy balloon catheters use inflated balloons to clear obstructions in blood vessels. Percutaneous thrombectomy devices facilitate the mechanical removal of clots through a minimally invasive technique, while catheter-guided thrombolysis devices deliver thrombolytic agents to dissolve clots effectively. Each device plays a crucial role in enhancing patient outcomes in thrombus management.</p></p>
