<p><p>Eyelink conveyor belts are categorized based on their construction and features. Full Eyelinks offer a continuous, sturdy design for heavy loads, while Pressed Eyelinks are made through a stamping process, providing cost-effective solutions. Welded Eyelinks feature strong joints for enhanced durability. Pressed and Welded Eyelinks combine both methods for added strength. Eyelinks with Springs allow for flexibility and adaptability in movement, while Eyelinks with Bushings or Washers minimize friction, improving efficiency and longevity in conveyor applications.</p></p>
