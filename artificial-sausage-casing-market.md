<p><p>The artificial sausage casing market comprises several types: collagen casing, cellulose casing, plastic casing, and others. Collagen casing is made from animal collagen and is favored for its strength and flexibility. Cellulose casing, derived from plant materials, is often used for skinless sausages and is not edible. Plastic casing is generally used for products requiring long shelf life and protection from moisture and air. Other casings can include various synthetic materials, catering to diverse processing and storage needs in the meat industry.</p></p>
