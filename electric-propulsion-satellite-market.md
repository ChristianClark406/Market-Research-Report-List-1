<p><ul><li>Gridded Ion Engine (GIE)</li><li>Hall Effect Thruster (HET)</li><li>High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thruster (HEMPT)</li><li>Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric propulsion satellite market features various technologies, including Gridded Ion Engines (GIE), which utilize electric fields to accelerate ions for thrust. Hall Effect Thrusters (HET) employ magnetic fields to ionize and propel gases efficiently. High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thrusters (HEMPT) enhance performance through multistage acceleration of plasma. Pulsed Plasma Thrusters (PPT) generate thrust by briefly discharging plasma, making them suitable for smaller satellite applications. Each technology offers distinct advantages in efficiency, thrust, and operational flexibility for space missions.</p></p>
