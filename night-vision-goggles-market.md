<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The night-vision goggles market primarily comprises two types: image intensifier and thermal imaging technologies. Image intensifier goggles enhance low-light conditions by amplifying available light, making them ideal for nighttime operations and activities in darkness. Conversely, thermal imaging goggles detect heat emitted by objects, allowing users to see in complete darkness or through obstructions like smoke or fog. Both technologies serve critical roles in military, law enforcement, and outdoor recreational applications, providing enhanced visibility in challenging environments.</p></p>
