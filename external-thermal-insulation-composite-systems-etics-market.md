<p><p>External Thermal Insulation Composite Systems (ETICS) are building envelope solutions designed to improve energy efficiency by providing thermal insulation on the exterior of walls. These systems comprise an insulated board, adhesive, and a robust reinforced finishing coat, which together create a continuous layer of insulation. ETICS are gaining popularity due to their ability to reduce energy consumption in buildings, thus contributing to sustainability goals and enhancing overall comfort.</p><p>The ETICS market is expected to witness significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient construction and renovation activities across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. As governments worldwide introduce stringent energy efficiency regulations and building codes, the adoption of ETICS is becoming more prevalent.
