<p><p>The ENT operating tables market features three primary types: electric, hydraulic, and manual operating tables. Hydraulic tables utilize fluid mechanisms for height and tilt adjustments, providing stability and flexibility during procedures. Manual operating tables require manual adjustments, which can be less convenient but often are more cost-effective. Each type serves distinct needs in ENT surgeries, catering to various hospital budgets and operational requirements.</p></p>
