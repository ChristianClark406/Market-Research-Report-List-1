<p><p>The micro inverter market can be categorized into single-phase and three-phase systems. Single-phase micro inverters are typically used in residential applications, providing efficient energy conversion for smaller solar setups by allowing individual panel optimization. In contrast, three-phase micro inverters cater to commercial or larger residential installations, enhancing performance and stability in higher power demand scenarios. Both types improve energy production by maximizing output from each solar panel, adapting to shading and orientation variations, and enabling detailed monitoring.</p></p>
