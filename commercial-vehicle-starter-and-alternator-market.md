<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Starter and Alternator Market is primarily divided into two main types: Alternators and Starter Motors. Alternators generate electricity to recharge the vehicle's battery and power onboard electrical systems while the engine is running. Starter motors, on the other hand, are responsible for initiating the engine's ignition process by cranking the engine. Both components are essential for the efficient operation of commercial vehicles, ensuring reliable starting and battery management to support various applications and extended vehicle use.</p></p>
