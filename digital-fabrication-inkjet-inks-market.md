Dye-based inks dissolve in liquid, offering vibrant colors and smooth finishes, making them ideal for applications requiring high clarity and brilliance. Conversely, pigment-based inks consist of solid particles suspended in a liquid, providing superior durability, UV resistance, and enhanced longevity on various substrates. Each type serves distinct applications in sectors such as textiles, packaging, and graphics, catering to specific user needs depending on environmental and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
