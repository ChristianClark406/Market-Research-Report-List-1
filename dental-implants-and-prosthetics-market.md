<p><p>Dental implants and prosthetics are specialized dental devices designed to replace missing teeth and restore functionality and aesthetics to patients. Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone, serving as anchors for artificial teeth. Prosthetics, including crowns, bridges, and dentures, are custom-made devices that attach to these implants or directly to the gum line to restore the natural appearance and function of teeth.</p><p>The market for dental implants and prosthetics is experiencing significant growth, propelled by factors such as the rising prevalence of dental diseases, increasing geriatric population, and advancements in dental technology. The demand for cosmetic dentistry and the growing popularity of dental tourism are further fueling market expansion.
