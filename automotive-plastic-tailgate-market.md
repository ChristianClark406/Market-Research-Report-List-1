<p><strong>The Automotive Plastic Tailgate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Tailgate</li><li>Thermoset Tailgate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive plastic tailgate market comprises two main types: thermoplastic and thermoset tailgates. Thermoplastic tailgates are lightweight, flexible, and can be reshaped upon heating, making them ideal for mass production and recycling. They offer better impact resistance and design versatility. In contrast, thermoset tailgates are known for their superior durability and heat resistance, providing enhanced structural integrity. However, they cannot be remolded after curing. Both types are increasingly used to meet automotive industry demands for efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
