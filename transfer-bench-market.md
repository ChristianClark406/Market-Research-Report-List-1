<p><p>Transfer benches are specialized seating solutions designed to assist individuals with mobility challenges during transfers in and out of bathtubs or showers. The two primary market types are retractable and ordinary transfer benches. Retractable transfer benches feature a folding design allowing for easy storage and adjustable length, catering to varying bathroom sizes. Ordinary transfer benches, on the other hand, are more rigid and fixed in length, providing stability and support but lack the flexibility of retractable models, making them suitable for consistent use.</p></p>
